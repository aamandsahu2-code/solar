Electrical Definitions (1)

\(P_{max}\)

Represents the highest wattage the panel can produce under specific test conditions.

\(I_{sc}\) (Short Circuit Current)

The max current a solar panel can produce when its + & - are directly connected (creating a Short Circuit).

\(V_{oc}\) (Voltage Open Circuit)

The max voltage a solar panel can produce when no electrical load is connected to it.

Temp & Temp IR

  • Temp IR: Infrared thermography used for temperature measurement and analysis.
  • Temp: Operating temp & impact on energy production.

Electrical Definitions (2)

Temperature Coefficients

  • Temp K: The temperature coefficient of power.
  • Temp PT1000: High precision temp sensor for backsheet.

MPP Parameters

  • \(I_{mp}\): Amperage at peak efficiency.
  • \(V_{mp}\): Voltage at peak power output.

Hipot Test: DCW & IR

1. DCW (Voltage Withstand)

  • Purpose: Check current leakage.
  • Formula: \(Sys \text{ Volt} \times 3 + 300V\)
  • Ex: \(1500 \times 3 + 300 = 4800V\)
  • Fail: Busbar/Matrix shift or frame touch.

2. IR (Insulation Resistance)

  • Purpose: Check Insulation.
  • Voltage: \(1500V\).
  • Fail: Busbar/Matrix shift or frame touch.

Hipot Test: GND & Specs

3. GND (Ground Continuity)

  • Purpose: Check resistance.
  • Formula: \(JB \text{ current} \times 2.5\)
  • Ex: \(30A \times 2.5 = 75A\).
  • Fail: Frame gap / JB short.

Key Formulas

1. Fill Factor (FF)

$$FF\% = \frac{P_{max}}{V_{oc} \times I_{sc}} \times 100$$ $$FF = \frac{I_{mp} \times V_{mp}}{V_{oc} \times I_{sc}}$$

Condition: \((>75\%)\)

2. Efficiency %

$$\eta\% = \frac{P_{max}}{\text{Area} \times \text{Irrad}} \times 100$$

Additional Formulas

3. \(I_{mp}\)

$$I_{mp} = P_{max} / V_{mp}$$

4. \(P_{max}\)

$$P_{max} = I_{mp} \times V_{mp}$$

5. Area of a Module

Formula: \(L \times W\)
